About

Hey there, I'm Morgan Kemp, a 25-year-old web developer based in the UK. I've got 24 months of hands-on development experience in both startup and corporate settings. I'm all about Linux, gaming, fitness and development! I'm here to showcase my story, experience and TechStack, welcome to my portfolio!

Currently I work for Paragon Bank within their mobile banking app team, using a wide range of languages and stacks.

Feel free to check out my work over on my GitHub!

Experience

In my inaugural professional role, I immersed myself in the fast-paced culture of startups, marking my entry into the field of web development immediately after graduating from university. This experience not only refined my coding prowess but also instilled a crucial understanding of the broader aspects of software development. I quickly learned that successful coding goes hand-in-hand with effective customer relations and seamless integrations, both pivotal for delivering a well-rounded user experience.

Transitioning into a corporate environment for my second role, I became an integral member of an internal development team. Here, my focus shifted towards leveraging low-code applications to contribute significantly to the creation of vital software solutions for the business. Working within the constraints of a compact team, we took on the challenge of developing large-scale applications, emphasizing the collaborative efforts essential for navigating the intricacies of corporate software development.

Tech Stack

In my quest to craft efficient solutions, I've curated a tech stack that combines reliability with innovation. Python is my trusty companion, versatile and readable, it's the backbone of many of my projects. When it comes to frontend design, I wield HTML and CSS to create interfaces that are both sleek and user-friendly.

On the backend, I'm all about maintaining order with SQL for database management, while turning to C++ for tackling complex operations with speed and precision. Navigating the Linux Terminal feels like second nature to me, providing a robust environment for development tasks. And let's not forget about data, JSON is my preferred tool for seamless information exchange, while Git keeps everything in sync with its version control prowess.

But I'm not content with just the basics. I've delved into the realm of OAPI creation for financial transactions, ensuring secure and efficient processing every step of the way. And then there's OutSystems, a platform where I thrive in building scalable applications with rapid development cycles. It's like having a superpower, making complex tasks feel like a breeze.

With this comprehensive tech stack at my disposal, I'm equipped to tackle a wide range of challenges, blending reliability with cutting-edge innovation for solutions that stand out from the crowd.
